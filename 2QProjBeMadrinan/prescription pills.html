Mental Disorders Symptoms Types
Anxiety disorders Constant and worsening anxiety Generalized Anxiety Disorder (GAD), Panic Disorder, Phobias
Mood disorders Unstable emotional state Bipolar Disorder, Depression
Eating disorders Severe abnormalities in eating behaviors Binge-eating, Bulimia Nervosa, Anorexia Nervosa
Personality disorders Unhealthy and inflexible long-term pattern of behavior Borderline Personality Disorder, Narcissistic Personality Disorder, Paranoia and Delusional DIsorders, Schizotypal Personality Disorder
Post-traumatic stress disorder Developed after experiencing trauma
Psychotic disorders Delusions, hallucinations, and abnormal thinking or perception Postpartum Psychosis, Schizoaffective Disorder, Schizophrenia

(Mental Disorders, 2013)

Hard Pill To Swallow

If you find yourself experiencing any symptoms of mental illness, consult a trusted psychiatrist. However, it's easier said than done. The first step to coping with mental disorders is to accept that you have one. It is difficult to suffer with a mental illness, but you are not alone in your struggles.

Remember

No matter the mental condition, we should always remember that neurodivergent people are still people. It is expected that we respect them and ensure that they are comfortable, but it does not mean we should see them as lesser than. They deserve the help they need as well as the respect given to anybody else.
